  • The Game of (Mediocre) Life

    A "mediocre" rendition of the classic board game: Game of Life.

    A light-hearted mockery of an ol' game classic. With 2-players possible, this game will sure to turn any dreary day into one filled with laughter (or at least with a chuckle or two).

    Objective:

    Here, in the game of LIFE, you will experience real-life scenarios that could either be beneficial or absolutely heartbreaking for your finances.

    LIFE, will not allow you to move back spaces, for no one can actually go back in time and undo the mistakes of the past...that's why it's called LIFE.

    Continuously move forward according to each roll spin until you reach the end (and hopefully the road won't be too painful).


    How to win:

    Whoever has the most amount of money by the end of the game wins.


    Made with:

    HTML/CSS3

    JavaScript

    Adobe Illustrator

  • Moodimizer

    Depending on their mood, this app is for the indecisive person to discover new adventures each day.

    Ever had those days where you just didn't know what you wanted to do? So you think and think about it...and then you end up staying at home not really doing anything and wasting your day away.
    Moodimizer takes that hassle of thinking really hard, and gives you plenty of suggestions on what to do based on what you're in the mood for! Now, THAT, is an surefire way to live life more adventurously!

    Goals:

    To reduce heavy flow of thoughts.

    For the indecisive person to live life adventurously but in an efficient way.


    Made with:

    Ruby on Rails

    HTML/CSS3

    Bootstrap3

    Adobe Illustrator/Photoshop

  • SpotBuds

    Awesome fitness app to find that awesome fitness buddy.

    Working hard or hardly working? Why not both?
    SpotBuds came together through a fitness vision, in hopes that the fitness community could grow in finding support and encouragement. In this app, it allows the user to find that that match-made-in-heaven fitness buddy, set a destination and time to workout at, and get pumped!

    Workflow:

    SpotBuds was a team-effort app created by Lewis Bracey, Nicole Thurnau, and Jennifer Wu


    Made with:

    Node.js

    Express

    MongoDB/Mongoose

    Materialize/CSS3

    GoogleMaps API

  • Cart.
